FAQ

Common questions

Is Muster really fully autonomous — will it send emails without any human?
Yes. In autonomous mode — the default — Muster sends replies from your shared inbox without any human interaction. The AI reads the incoming question, retrieves the relevant policy passage, drafts a reply, and sends it. You review the audit log on your own schedule. If you prefer oversight during an initial period, switch to review mode until you're comfortable, then flip back.
How do reviewers approve in review mode?
Two ways: in the Muster web app (dashboard → Pending), or by replying to the approval email that Muster sends to the reviewer. The email contains the draft, the source policy excerpt, and approve/reject links — no login required. Either method records the reviewer's identity in the audit log.
What happens when Muster can't find a matching policy?
If the retrieval confidence falls below your configured threshold, Muster does not send — in either mode. Instead it flags the email for human handling and notifies the inbox owner. This means Muster never guesses or fabricates an answer.
Is employee data used to train the AI models?
No. Muster uses the Anthropic API under a zero-data-retention agreement — inputs and outputs are not used for model training. Your policy documents are stored in your own M365 tenant and processed in your selected data residency region.
Can I use Muster without SharePoint?
Yes. You can upload PDF and Word documents directly to the Muster knowledge base, or link OneDrive folders. SharePoint auto-sync is a convenience feature, not a requirement.
How long does it take to set up?
The technical setup takes one day. The M365 app registration is about 20 minutes; indexing your first policy documents is another 10–30 minutes. Getting your knowledge base production-ready — making sure policies are current, clear, and complete — typically takes 1–2 additional weeks. We help you audit and prepare your documents as part of onboarding.
How long before we can switch to fully autonomous mode?
Most teams run in review mode for 2–4 weeks — long enough to see a representative sample of questions and verify the replies are accurate. Some teams go autonomous on day one once they've confirmed the knowledge base quality. Switching is a one-click toggle, per inbox. There's no re-setup involved.
Do you help with setup and onboarding?
Yes. Full onboarding, policy audit, and knowledge base preparation are included in every plan. We help you review your existing documents, flag ambiguous or outdated passages, and ensure the knowledge base is accurate before going live. You're not left to figure it out alone.
What's the timeline from signing to first autonomous reply?
Technical setup takes one day — M365 app registration is about 20 minutes. Preparing your knowledge base (auditing and uploading your policy documents) typically takes 1–2 weeks depending on how current your documents are. Most teams send their first autonomous reply in week 2–3 after signing.
